About Our Project

Our data provides a visualization of ICU beds in USA and covid-19 cases in the month of March 2020.

The map show the population, ICU beds.

The line graphs and pie chart depicts covid-19 confirmed, deaths and recovered cases in March 2020. The pie chart will depict the ration of confirmed, recoverd and death cases within a county
